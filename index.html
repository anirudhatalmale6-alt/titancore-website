❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TitanCore. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our team.

How long does implementation take?

+
Most organizations are fully onboarded within 2-4 weeks. Our dedicated implementation team handles data migration, configuration, and training. For larger enterprises with complex requirements, we offer phased rollouts that can extend to 6-8 weeks.

Is TitanCore compliant with UAE labor laws?

+
Absolutely. TitanCore is built specifically for the UAE and GCC market. We support WPS (Wage Protection System) compliance, gratuity calculations per UAE labor law, visa and PRO documentation tracking, and automatic updates whenever regulations change.

Can I migrate from my current HR system?

+
Yes, we support migration from all major HRMS platforms. Our team will assist with data mapping, cleansing, and importing your employee records, leave balances, payroll history, and documents. We've successfully migrated companies with over 5,000 employees.

Is my data secure?

+
Security is our top priority. TitanCore uses AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, and hosts data in UAE-based data centers. We perform regular penetration testing and offer role-based access controls at every level.

Do you offer a free trial?

+
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of our Professional plan with no credit card required. You get full access to all features including payroll, attendance, and the mobile app. Our team will help you set up sample data so you can see TitanCore in action immediately.

What kind of support do you provide?

+
All plans include email and chat support. Professional plans get priority support with under 4-hour response times. Enterprise clients receive a dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone support, and on-site training sessions for your team.
